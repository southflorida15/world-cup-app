# Product Vision

World Cup App is a personalized companion for following the FIFA World Cup.

It is not trying to become a generic sports news app. It is built around the user's World Cup: favorite teams, live matches, fantasy picks, brackets, stats and match context.

## North Star

The app should feel like a TV broadcast control room in your pocket.

## Core values

- Personalized.
- Live.
- Fast.
- Mobile-first.
- Clear.
- Useful before tapping.
- Fun without being gimmicky.
- Reliable during matchdays.

## Product promise

When a user opens the app, they should know in under five seconds:

- what just happened,
- what is happening now,
- what comes next,
- how their teams are doing,
- whether their fantasy or bracket needs attention.

## Signature experience

My WC is the app's personalized entry point.

It should always prioritize:

1. Last Matches.
2. Live Matches or Today.
3. Next Matches.
4. Fantasy Rank.
5. Top Scorers.
6. Team Watch.

## Success metrics

- User opens the app multiple times per matchday.
- User reaches important match context in one tap.
- User can understand the current tournament state without searching.
- User trusts that live stats and scorers are consistent across screens.
