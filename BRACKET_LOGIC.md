# World Cup App – Bracket Logic

## Formats
- Actual Bracket = real tournament.
- My Bracket = user simulation.

## FIFA 2026
- 12 groups.
- Top 2 qualify automatically.
- Best 8 third-placed teams qualify.
- Annex C determines Round of 32 placement.

## Circular Bracket
The circular bracket must always use the same knockout graph as the tree bracket.
Only the visual positioning differs.

Never maintain a separate tournament logic for the circular view.
