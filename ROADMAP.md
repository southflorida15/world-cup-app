Priorities may evolve as the app matures, live data improves, and new tournament requirements emerge.

## Guiding Product Principles

1. **Accuracy first**  
   Tournament structure and match data should be as accurate as possible.

2. **Useful for real fans**  
   Every feature should help someone follow, understand, or enjoy the World Cup.

3. **Polished experience**  
   The app should feel intentional, modern, and easy to use.

4. **Documentation matters**  
   The repository should be as clear and professional as the app itself.

5. **Build safely**  
   Improve the app without breaking production.
